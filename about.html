Early years of experience

Born in Hong Kong, China, Wong Ka Yee fell in love with fencing at the age of 10 after first coming into contact with it in a primary school hobby class. He won an individual gold medal in the men's 17-year-old category at the Asian Youth Championships at the age of 17, and won three Asian titles, three national titles and nine international and Hong Kong titles during his career. In 2011, he travelled to Korea to become a trainee and spent two and a half years as a trainee. In September 2013, he participated in the recording of the trainee debut show "Win: Who is next", where he competed with YG's trainees in a skills competition.
